Depressive Symptoms and Hypertension Among U.S. Adults

An epidemiologic analysis using NHANES data to examine the relationship between depressive symptoms and hypertension among U.S. adults, with a focus on Asian American adults. The project includes data cleaning, descriptive analysis, regression modeling, and interpretation of population-level findings.

A public health research project examining barriers and facilitators that affect cervical cancer screening in clinical settings.
